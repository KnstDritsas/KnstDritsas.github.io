    My Journey with IEEE Student Branch

    Date: May 2022 - Nov 2022

    During this period, as a founding member of the IEEE Student Branch at the University of Peloponnese-Patras, I played a pivotal role in establishing the organizational structure and promoting awareness of our activities. Together with fellow members, I contributed to drafting the branch constitution and creating promotional content. We organized meetings to engage with members, voted on the articles of the constitution, and conducted team bonding activities to foster a sense of community.

    Date: Nov 2022 - Present: Vice Chair

    As Vice Chair of the IEEE Student Branch, I have focused on enhancing our online presence and organizational efficiency. One of my key contributions was creating the branch website, improving accessibility and engagement. Additionally, I've continued to organize meetings and activities to promote IEEE within the university and wider community, emphasizing the benefits of IEEE membership.

    Date: May 2023 - Present: Organizer of the 1st Summer School on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

    In May 2023, I organized the inaugural Summer School on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at the University of Peloponnese. Over the course of three days, participants gained valuable insights from esteemed speakers and engaged in practical sessions to deepen their understanding of AI and ML. My responsibilities included planning the program, coordinating with the university's Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, inviting speakers, managing logistics, and evaluating feedback to ensure the success of the event.
